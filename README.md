# Tic Tac Toe

## Game Concept 
A standard 2-player game of tic tac tie. Use your mouse or trackpad to navigate to get 3 in a row before your opponent. 

## Gameplay 
* Click "Start Game" to generate board. 
* Player 1: Use mouse or trackpad to select your square. You will be X to start.
* Player 2: After player 1's turn, use mouse ro trackpad to slect your square. You will be O to start.
* Contiue in the fashion until one player gets three in a row or there is a tie. Results will be displayed at the end of the round.
* Play multiple rounds by clicking "Restart Game" and following the above steps again.
